Our Journey Through Time

At John Bitar and Co. Ltd., our history is deeply rooted in the rich tradition of timber craftsmanship. Since our humble beginnings, we have been on a journey of growth, innovation, and a commitment to excellence.

John Bitar, a Ghanaian of Lebanese origin, started his journey as a cocoa crop farmer in the Brong Ahafo region and went on to establish John Bitar & Co. Ltd (JCM) in 1955.

Today, the company stands as Ghana's premier producer of premium-quality wood products under the Free Zones Initiative. Operating two extensive manufacturing facilities, we specialize in crafting various products from the finest tropical hardwoods and softwoods.

Our core operations encompass logging, sawmilling, and veneer activities, supplying high-grade tropical hardwood timber under the renowned trademarks ''JCM'' and ''SUMA'' to esteemed companies across Europe, North America, the Middle East, Asia, and Africa. Our diverse product range includes Sliced Veneers, Rotary Cut Veneers, Plywood, Lumber, Mouldings, profiled sections, Laminated and Finger-jointed products, and Flooring.

As a family business, Ghassan John Bitar has assumed management responsibilities from the founder due to the passing of time. Our company is headquartered in Mempeasem (Sekondi) in the Western Region and the Suhuma facility in Sefwi Dwinase, Western North Region of Ghana.
