Sustainable Timber Sourcing

At John Bitar & Co. Ltd., we are committed to sourcing timber sustainably, ensuring environmental responsibility and supporting communities.

Responsibly Managed Forests

Our timber products come from forests that adhere to sustainable forestry practices, promoting biodiversity and minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainable Practices

We work closely with local communities, supporting economic development and ensuring that our timber sourcing positively impacts the people and regions involved.

Our Certifications

We hold the following certifications to ensure the highest standards of sustainability:

  • Forest Stewardship Council (FSC)
  • Programme for the Endorsement of Forest Certification (PEFC)

Partnerships

We collaborate with organizations and initiatives that share our vision for sustainable timber sourcing:

  • Global Timber Alliance
  • Environmental Timber Foundation
