Our Mission

The core mission of our company is to maximize the utilization of all available species within Timber Contract Areas, encompassing both forest and off-reserve domains. We are dedicated to prioritizing the promotion of lesser-used species, emphasizing quality over quantity, and adding substantial value to our products to enhance returns, contributing significantly to vital foreign exchange earnings.

Our Core Values

  • Contribution towards Afforestation
  • Reforestation in degraded forest areas
  • Commitment to Corporate Social Responsibility
  • In addition, John Bitar value behaviours that support its core values and which have been embedded in the company management system namely Integrity, Innovation, Teamwork, and Ethics
